About

Akshay

Akshay is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts, working across digital illustration, ink, and watercolor. His sketchbooks travel everywhere with him — street vendors, family afternoons, plants on a balcony — while his digital work leans into warmer, painterly portraiture and the occasional bit of fantasy.

He posts as @artofakshay_, where the sketchbook pages, process shots, and finished pieces all live side by side.
